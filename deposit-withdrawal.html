Methods Important Information

1. After receiving the withdrawal request form, it will be processed within 48 hours. Usually, it is posted on the same day!

2. Due to third-party trading rules, funds can only be refunded to a bank account with the same name (or joint name) as your FY-VIEW trading account.

3. Any international telegraphic transfer (TT) fees charged by our banking institutions are passed on to customers.

4. Recharge through bank wire transfer usually takes about 10 minutes for the funds to reach your account.

5. Please note that in the event of any unforeseen circumstances, withdrawals may take longer.
